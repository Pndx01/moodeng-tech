Who is R&F?

Ramil & Friends (R&F) was established in 2025 as a small business by Noriel, Rengiee, Belle, Dave, and Jeff alongside Ramil.

The "Friends" in Ramil & Friends isn't just branding. We promise to approach every client relationship with trust, transparency, and genuine care. We're here for the long haul, building relationships that extend beyond a single repair.

Our Promise

Behind every laptop is a person with deadlines to meet, memories to preserve, or work that can't wait. We never forget that, and it's this understanding that sets us apart.

Introducing Moodeng Tech

Moodeng Tech is our specialized laptop repair service, and it's where our expertise truly shines. By focusing exclusively on laptops, we've developed deep knowledge of these machines: every model, every common issue, every quirk.

Our Focus

We specialize ONLY in laptops. This exclusive focus means we know laptops better than anyone. No distractions, no generalists - just pure laptop expertise.

Our Approach

We keep things refreshingly straightforward: honest diagnostics, transparent pricing, clear communication, and personalized attention to every client.
