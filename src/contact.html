Quick Answers

What's the best way to contact you?

Message us on Facebook (Moodeng Tech) for the quickest response, or fill out our contact form above. We check messages regularly during business hours (8 AM - 10 PM).

How quickly will you respond?

We typically respond within a few hours during business hours. If you reach out after hours, we'll get back to you first thing the next day.

Can I drop off my laptop without an appointment?

Yes! We accept walk-ins during business hours (8 AM - 10 PM, daily). However, calling ahead is appreciated so we can prioritize your service.

What information should I provide about my laptop?

Share the brand/model, what's not working, and any error messages you're seeing. Photos of the damage help too!

Do you offer emergency repairs?

We work hard to accommodate urgent repairs within our business hours. Contact us immediately if you have an emergency.
