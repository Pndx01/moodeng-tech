Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

It depends on the issue. Express repairs (RAM upgrades, SSD installation) take 30-60 minutes. Complex repairs vary, but we always provide honest timelines.

What's the warranty on repairs?

All repairs come with a warranty. Ask our team about specific warranty details when you drop off your laptop.

Can I watch my repair?

Yes! We offer optional live camera feed so you can watch your repair in progress if you'd like.

What if parts need to be ordered?

We'll contact you immediately and provide an honest timeline. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

How do I get my Repair ID?

You'll receive your Repair ID via SMS and email when you drop off your laptop at our shop.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We perform automatic backups before any repair (with your consent) and handle your data with complete care.

Can't Find Your Repair?

Contact us directly and we'll help you track it down.
